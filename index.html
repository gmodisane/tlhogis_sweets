The story behind the bakes

Meet Tlhogi

Tlhogi trained properly, taking baking classes to sharpen her craft and learn the techniques behind every great cake. That foundation, paired with a deep personal passion, quickly became something her friends, family, and their networks couldn't stop talking about. Word spread the old fashioned way, someone tasted something and couldn't stop thinking about it.

Today, Tlhogi's Sweets & Treats serves the greater Johannesburg community from her home kitchen, where every order gets her full, undivided attention. No shortcuts, no fuss, just beautiful bakes that arrive looking like they were made for the occasion, because they were.

Think Woolworths quality, but personal. Think a gift that says I thought of you before a single word is spoken.
